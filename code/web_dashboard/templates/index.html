About Smart Orb

The Smart Orb is a revolutionary wearable device that integrates cutting-edge technologies to enhance physical performance, facilitate recovery, and improve overall well-being.

Based on extensive research in neurostimulation, electrochemical impedance analysis, and multi-sensory integration, the Smart Orb delivers personalized experiences that adapt to your unique physiology and needs.

Developed by Ucaretron Inc., the Smart Orb technology is protected under Patent Application No. KR10-2024-0071235.
